Odin’s Smoked Elk Stew

A hearty Viking-style stew made with smoked elk, root vegetables, and wild herbs.

Jump to Recipe
Prep time: 25 mins
Cook time: 3 hrs
Servings: 6 warriors
Difficulty: Heroic

Ingredients

  • 800g smoked elk (or beef stew meat)
  • 2 large carrots, chopped
  • 3 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 1 tsp crushed juniper berries
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter or animal fat

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat and sauté onions and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add elk meat and brown on all sides.
  3. Add carrots and potatoes, then pour in the beef stock.
  4. Stir in thyme and juniper berries. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer covered for 2.5 to 3 hours, stirring occasionally.
  6. Remove the lid for the last 30 minutes to thicken the stew.
  7. Serve hot with rustic bread and mead (optional but highly recommended).

Notes

If elk is hard to find, high-quality beef works well. This stew tastes even better the next day after the flavors have deepened.
