Odin’s Roast Feast

A mouth-watering roasted meat platter that honors the Allfather himself. Perfect for a feast worthy of the gods.

Jump to Recipe
Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 2 hrs
Servings: 6 people
Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 kg mixed cuts of beef, pork, and lamb
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 4 carrots, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large bowl, combine olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, rosemary, and thyme.
  3. Rub the mixture thoroughly over the meat cuts.
  4. Place the onions and carrots at the bottom of a roasting pan.
  5. Arrange the meat on top of the vegetables.
  6. Pour the beef broth over the meat and vegetables.
  7. Cover the pan with foil and roast for 1.5 hours.
  8. Remove the foil and roast for another 30 minutes until the meat is browned.
  9. Remove from oven and let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  10. Slice and serve with the roasted vegetables and pan juices.

Notes

For best results, use fresh herbs and quality meat cuts. You can substitute the vegetables for your favorites. This dish pairs wonderfully with a hearty red wine.
