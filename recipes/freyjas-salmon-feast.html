Freyja’s Salmon Feast

A divine Nordic salmon dish with herbs, lemon, and a touch of magic—worthy of the goddess herself.

Jump to Recipe
Prep time: 15 mins
Cook time: 20 mins
Servings: 4 chosen guests
Difficulty: Graceful

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (approx. 180g each)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: edible flowers for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and rub with garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Top each fillet with lemon slices and sprinkle with dill and parsley.
  5. Bake for 15–20 minutes, until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Serve hot, optionally garnished with edible flowers for a truly divine touch.

Notes

This dish pairs beautifully with roasted root vegetables or wild rice. A crisp white wine or honeyed mead enhances the flavors wonderfully.
